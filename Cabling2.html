Cabling

A cable defines a set of cables that can transmit data from one place to another. In the case of CCTV, it involves installation and providing connection between the cables that help to establish the transmission of visual data along with audio for recording or monitoring purposes. Enheal has started providing services in terms of CCTV and hence makes us unique from other service providers. We have our staffs that have expertise in such field. Thus, there are some points to discuss in cables. There are two types of cables used in the field of CCTV. Those are:-

  • Coaxial cables - Coaxial cables are used for transmitting high-quality video over a long distance. These cables are being used for analog CCTV systems. They are much easier to install as compared to twisted pair cables. These types of cables are being used for providing power to the camera and transmit high-quality video.
  • Twisted Pair cables - Twisted pair cables are used for IP-based CCTV systems. These kind of cables are being able to transmit video and power over the ethernet that helps in installation process. These cables has the tendency to transmit data over long distances with ease.
