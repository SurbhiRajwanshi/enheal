Rack Installation (Cabling)

It is the type of arrangement that takes place in the cabling in which racks are being provided and all the cables are being managed inside them. These racks are available for servers, devices, cabinets, etc. There are some important points in installing a rack. Those are:-

Equipment size - The size of racks can vary depending on the usage. Like if you use it for a device, then racks are in bigger sizes, and for cables, those are much smaller compared to other racks.

Types of racks - There are various types of racks like open-frame racks, wall-mounted racks, and closed-cabinets racks. Enheal technicians will provide proper information about the type of rack they are using and there is always room for listening to the customer’s idea.

Management - The racks are being installed for the management of all the things in one place but cables are the one that is difficult to manage and for that, we use those racks which have completely different sections for the cables in the rack itself.

Power distribution - One of the most important is the power distribution ins the racks. Racks are the ones that can be installed by anyone but very few have the expertise to manage the power distribution. But working with Enheal, none of the customers has ever complained about the voltage fluctuation or MCB being tripped. We recruit only staff who can work at their full potential without creating room for error.

Ventilation - As racks are comprised of numerous devices so the issue of heating takes place and there is no solution for this. But the Enheal technician advises you to install AC in the rooms where racks are placed so that minimum heating issue is inflicted and no wear and tear cases of devices should be there in future.
