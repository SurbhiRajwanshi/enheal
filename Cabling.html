Cabling

Cabling in networking defines as wires that are used to connect devices and through which data is being transmitted. It is one of the most important parts of networking. Enheal technicians that visit and install the devices are capable of cabling in all the devices and wherever necessary. There are some factors in cabling. Those are:-

Types of cables - These are further categories as

Ethernet cables - These are the cables in which the LAN network grind is present. These can transmit data up to 1 Gbps.

Fiber Optic cables - These are the cables that are used for transmitting data to longer distances. These types of cables are used in Wi-Fi.

Cable Management - Enheal technicians are so experienced that they can manage the cables in such a manner that it does feel messy. Like in the case of rack installation, there are huge networks of cables and technicians can manage them via zip ties for future betterment.

Testing - Once the cables are installed, then testing is done. In testing, the technician tests that the cables can transmit data.
